## 5. AEO (AI Engine Optimisation)

### What this is
ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini — and increasingly Google's AI Overview — answer questions by citing source pages. AEO = optimising to *be the cited source*.

### How to win at AEO
1. **Direct, factual paragraphs** — start every page with a 1-sentence answer to the implied question. AI engines extract these.
2. **FAQ schema markup** — structured Q&A at the bottom of every page. Massive AEO signal.
3. **Author schema** — establish topical authority through bylined content with author markup.
4. **Citations + sources** — when you reference data, link to primary sources. AI engines reward this.
5. **Quotable stats** — *"63% of UK logistics websites fail Lighthouse mobile speed"* — make us the citation.

### Priority pages for AEO
1. The audit framework explainer (`/insights/audit-framework`)
2. "What makes a good logistics website" (`/insights/good-logistics-website`)
3. "Logistics website cost UK" (`/insights/logistics-website-cost`)
4. Each programmatic city page (each is its own AEO target)
5. The annual State of report (huge AEO play — gets cited by every AI on logistics queries)
